Speed Cola usually means the difference between life and death when surrounded by zombies. If gives you a sense of limitless energy, which translates into effortlessly swapping weapons and reloading faster than the eyes can follow. Unfortuneately, it has some detrimental effects on your health. Survivors of Kino der Toten noted that repeated use will rot out even the healthiest of teeth. And the damage caused on a human cardiovascular system is not fully understood. But desperate times call for desperate measures! This is a staple of survival in high intensity situations. The ingrediants are listed below:

  • Carbonated Water
  • Glucose
  • Cola Nut Extract
  • Hydrolized Crank
  • Caffeine
  • Guarana
  • Taurine
  • Cayenne Pepper

    • All ingrediants must be added in the order listed above, at a ration of 90% of the previous ingrediant. Mix together thoroughly for 2 minutes at 76 degrees Fahrenheit. Immediately refrigerate for 2 hours. Ready for consumption. Good luck out there Survivor!
